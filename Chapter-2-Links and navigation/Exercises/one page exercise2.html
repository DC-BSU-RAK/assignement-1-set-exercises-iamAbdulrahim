Charlie and the Chocolate Factory

*Charlie and the Chocolate Factory* is a fantasy film based on the 1964 novel by Roald Dahl. It tells the story of Charlie Bucket, a kind-hearted boy from a poor family who wins a tour of the mysterious and magical chocolate factory run by the eccentric Willy Wonka.

Cast & Creative Team

  • Director: Tim Burton
  • Willy Wonka: Johnny Depp
  • Charlie Bucket: Freddie Highmore
  • Screenplay: John August
  • Music: Danny Elfman

Main Characters

  • Charlie Bucket :Honest, kind, and humble boy.
  • Willy Wonka :Reclusive and quirky chocolate factory owner.
  • Veruca Salt : Spoiled and demanding girl.
  • Augustus Gloop : Greedy boy who loves food.
  • Violet Beauregarde : Gum-chewing champion.
  • Mike Teavee : Television-obsessed kid.
